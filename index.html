About Me

Hi, I'm Rachel!

My earliest memories include me running around with a camera in hand capturing memories of friends, family, and pets. The time between now and then has been spent advancing my passion into something tangible so I can continue to do what I love for other people!

Photography was never about me, it was about the person or people I was taking a picture of. And I'd love to share that with you!

I'm currently based in St. Pete, Florida. I love local, outdoor sessions but will also travel for couples, engagements, elopments, family, and pet photography sessions. The confidence and comfort of my clients and models are the highest priority so we can make the best of our time together!
